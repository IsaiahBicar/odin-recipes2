Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

This is my favortie type of cookie. I love the taste of the chocolate mixed in with the cookie. Especially if the cookie is freshley baked out of the oven. In this recipe I will teach you how to make the perfect chocolate chip cookie

Note that I will not be listing out proportions because the website I am looking at does not have proportions listed

Ingridients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips (Obvious)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.
